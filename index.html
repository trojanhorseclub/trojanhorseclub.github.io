About

TROJAN HORSE is Bruno Santos, Paloma Moniz, Ricardo Gonçalves and Roxy Zeiher. We are a collective of designers based between Berlin, Lisbon and London. Formed during our studies in Portugal and the UK, we have developed a research-driven practice focused on collaboration and criticality. We take a multidisciplinary approach to communication, one that aims to create exciting conceptual frameworks to be applied in editorial projects, visual identities, digital design and exhibition and spatial design.

Through subversive and deconstructive methodologies, we design towards unexpected results. With knowledge of typography, bookmaking, photography, video, programming, and generative models, we deploy an experimental approach to engage with projects that relate to a wider contemporary cultural and social landscape.
